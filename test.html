This week I recommend: Lübeck

Lübeck, my dream city in Germany!

Lübeck is a northern German city known for its Brick Gothic architecture, dating back to its time as the medieval capital of the Hanseatic League.

The Hanseatic city of Lübeck is located by the Baltic Sea and is known for its beautiful old town, a UNESCO World Heritage site.
